About Us

NayePankh Foundation works for education, women empowerment, and child welfare. It is a non-governmental organization with a strong desire to help society and make it a better place for all.

  • Distributing food to underprivileged communities
  • Providing sanitary napkins and hygiene awareness
  • Donating clothes to poor families
  • Running internship programs for 30,000+ students
